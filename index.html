Policies

These policies exist to protect the client first, and to keep execution clean and fair. We will refine and expand policies over time.

Pay After Service (Overview)
  • Client safety comes first. We structure the trip to reduce risk and increase confidence.
  • When the client chooses a full package model, payment is completed after the trip once services are delivered as agreed.
  • We focus on clarity (scope), discipline (execution), and transparency (pricing).
Alarab Access Fee (10%)
  • Access Fee is a transparent service fee equal to 10% of the agreed total.
  • The 10% is paid upfront by card to confirm and activate the arrangement.
  • The client pays the hotel directly at check-in / arrival (when applicable).
  • If a cancellation occurs, the paid amount is returned within 5 business days (based on the cancellation terms).
Data & Privacy
  • We protect client information and restrict access internally.
  • We do not share private client data beyond what is necessary to deliver the service.
Detailed terms (cancellations, changes, and service scope by category) will be published as we expand the system.
